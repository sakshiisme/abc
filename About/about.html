About Us

AADINATH BOX CORPORATION is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality corrugated boxes and packaging solutions. With our tagline "Sustainable Packaging Solutions," we are committed to offering eco-friendly, durable, and cost-effective packaging products to meet the needs of a diverse clientele. Since our establishment in 2005, we have specialized in producing corrugated boxes that are both environmentally friendly and customizable for industries such as e-commerce, retail, food & beverage, electronics, and logistics.
